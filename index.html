What is Jubensha?

Jubensha (剧本杀) is a social deduction game where players take on character roles to solve mysteries through investigation, discussion, and deduction.

  • 3-8 players per game
  • 2-4 hours gameplay
  • Multiple storylines available
  • Perfect for team building
