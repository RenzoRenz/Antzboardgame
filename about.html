About AntzBoardgame

Your premier destination for board games and mystery adventures in Miri

Our Story

Welcome to AntzBoardgame, Miri's premier destination for board game enthusiasts! We're passionate about bringing people together through the joy of gaming, creating memorable experiences one game at a time.

Founded with a vision to create a welcoming space for gamers of all levels, we offer an extensive collection of board games, from classic family favorites to modern strategy masterpieces and immersive Jubensha experiences.
