What is Jubensha?

Jubensha (剧本杀) is a social deduction game where players take on character roles to solve mysteries through investigation, discussion, and deduction.

  • 3-8 players per game
  • 2-4 hours gameplay
  • Multiple storylines available
  • Perfect for team building

Available Themes

Mystery & Crime

Classic detective stories with intricate plots

Horror & Thriller

Spine-chilling adventures for brave souls

Historical

Period-based mysteries from different eras

Fantasy

Magical world scenarios with supernatural elements

How to Play

1

Choose Your Character

Each player receives a character with unique background and secrets

2

Investigate

Search for clues, examine evidence, and gather information

3

Discuss & Deduce

Share findings, debate theories, and identify the culprit

4

Reveal Truth

Present your conclusions and discover the real story
